WHAT THIS SYSTEM DOES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Daily (3:45 PM ET):
  1. Analyzes 40+ stocks using technical indicators
  2. Selects best stock for OPTIONS trading
  3. Suggests CALL or PUT option contract
  4. Posts suggestion to Discord

Every 15 Minutes (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET):
  1. Monitors active OPTIONS positions
  2. Tracks option P/L (using Black-Scholes)
  3. Closes when target/stop/expiry hit
  4. Logs all trades to journal

This is ONLY for options trading. NOT for stock trading.
